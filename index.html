Our Services

Specialized Physiotherapy Services

Orthopedic and Sports Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy assessment and treatment for musculoskeletal and sports injuries in Kanata and Stittsville, Ottawa. We provide care for a wide range of conditions including sprains, strains, arthritis, and chronic pain.

Pelvic Floor Physiotherapy

Pelvic Floor Physiotherapy for Incontinence, prolapse, and pelvic pain management in Kanata and Stittsville, Ottawa. Specialized care for all life stages with compassionate, individualized approach.

EDS/ HSD Hypermobility Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy for Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome and hypermobility spectrum disorders in Kanata and Stittsville, Ottawa. Gentle, personalized care focused on joint stability and pain reduction.

Prenatal and Postnatal Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy for pregnancy and postpartum recovery in Kanata and Stittsville. Safe, tailored care including birth preparation and postnatal healing support.

Pessary Fitting for Prolapse and Incontinence

Pessary fitting Physiotherapy and follow-up care for prolapse and incontinence in Kanata and Stittsville, Ottawa. Non-surgical, effective support for pelvic health.

Dry Needling and Cupping

Physiotherapy techniques using needles and cups for pain relief, scar mobilization and muscle recovery in Kanata and Stittsville, Ottawa.

Laser Therapy

Physiotherapy using Non-invasive LASER treatment for pain and inflammation in Kanata and Stittsville, Ottawa. Gentle treatment to stimulate natural healing processes.

Virtual Physiotherapy

Online physiotherapy sessions at your convenience across Ontario. Compassionate care from the comfort and safety of your own home.
